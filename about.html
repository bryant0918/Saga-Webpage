About Us

At Family Saga, we believe every family has a story worth preserving. Our mission is to help you turn those stories into beautifully crafted charts that celebrate your heritage and keep your history alive for generations to come.

Family Saga is led by Tom Eichelberger, a passionate genealogist with a Genealogy Certificate from Boston University and over seven years of professional experience at Ancestry. Tom has spent years helping thousands of families uncover their origins, connect missing branches, and bring ancestral stories to light with accuracy and heart.

Standing alongside Tom is his brother-in-law, Bryant McArthur, Family Saga's technology specialist. Bryant keeps everything running smoothly behind the scenes—ensuring our systems, tools, and digital processes stay fast, reliable, and ready to support our clients as they explore their family history.

Our charts are not just names on a page—each ancestor is represented with a photo whenever possible, because a picture truly brings them to life. Seeing their faces transforms your family history into something powerful, personal, and unforgettable.

With a unique blend of research expertise, storytelling, and design, Family Saga creates personalized family charts that don't just display your lineage—they make your heritage feel real and vivid.
